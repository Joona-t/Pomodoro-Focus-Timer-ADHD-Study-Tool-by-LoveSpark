# Privacy Policy for Pomodoro Focus Timer & ADHD Study Tool by LoveSpark

**Last Updated:** March 5, 2026

## Overview

LoveSpark Focus is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.** LoveSpark Focus does not:
- Track your browsing activity
- Collect personally identifiable information
- Store your browsing history
- Transmit any data to external servers
- Use analytics or tracking services
- Require an account or login

## Local Storage

LoveSpark Focus stores data locally on your device using your browser's storage APIs. This includes:
- Timer settings (focus duration, break duration, intervals)
- Task list (task names, completion status, pomodoro estimates)
- Session statistics (sessions completed, focus minutes, streaks)
- Blocked site list (domains you choose to block during focus)
- UI preferences (theme, overlay position, sound settings)

**This data:**
- Never leaves your device
- Is not accessible to us or any third parties
- Can be cleared by uninstalling the extension or clearing your browser data

## Permissions

LoveSpark Focus requires the following permissions:

- **Storage:** To save your timer settings, tasks, statistics, and preferences locally on your device
- **Alarms:** To schedule reliable timer countdowns that work even when the browser is in the background
- **DeclarativeNetRequest:** To block distracting websites you configure during active focus sessions
- **Notifications:** To alert you when a focus or break session ends
- **Host permissions (all URLs):** To display the floating timer overlay on any page and to apply site blocking rules to any domain in your blocklist

No other permissions are requested.

## Third-Party Services

LoveSpark Focus does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools. All functionality runs entirely on your device.
